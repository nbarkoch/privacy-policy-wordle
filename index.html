Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how our Wordle game application ("the App") handles information. Our App is designed to be primarily offline and family-friendly, suitable for users of all ages including children under 13.

Key Points:
  • The App primarily functions offline
  • We do not collect or store personal information
  • Game progress is stored only on your device
  • Advertisements are non-personalized for all users

Information Collection and Use

Game Data

The App stores only:

  • Game scores and progress (stored locally on your device only)
  • Game settings and preferences (stored locally on your device only)

Offline Functionality

The App is designed to work offline. The only online feature is the display of advertisements.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display non-personalized advertisements in our App. For child safety:

  • All ads are non-personalized
  • We use family-friendly ad content only
  • No behavioral targeting is used
  • No personal information is collected for advertising purposes

Google AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device information (like device type and operating system)
  • IP address (for basic functionality only)
  • Non-personal usage data

For more information about Google AdMob's data practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect or store any data on external servers
  • You can delete all stored data by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

Our App is designed to be child-friendly and compliant with children's privacy laws (including COPPA). We:

  • Do not collect personal information from any users, including children
  • Do not track user behavior
  • Use only non-personalized advertisements
  • Store game progress locally on the device only

Parents can control app data by:

  • Clearing the app data through device settings
  • Uninstalling the app to remove all local data
  • Using device-level parental controls for app access and ad restrictions

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please contact us at: pumpedita3@gmail.com

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your device's locally stored game data
  • Delete all app data from your device
  • Control ad settings through your device settings
